My music composition app, CompSmart!

CompSmart

CompSmart is a cross-platform music composition app that specializes in chord suggestions. Users can upload audio files containing a melody, and the chordal suggestion feature will recommend appropriate chords that fit the individual measures of the melody. After selecting the desired chords, CompSmart renders a new audio file containing the original melody accompanied by the selected chords. Users then have the option to play it and save it to their device for future access.
